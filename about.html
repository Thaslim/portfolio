About Me

At the end of 2018, I started thinking of what I wanted next after having two years of maternity break in my career.[One of the best decision I took]. After some serious self-reflection and a month long research about the latest technologies, I decided to pursue my Master's degree in Computer Engineering. Within two months I took GRE & TOEFL exams and started my application process. Finally I got accepted into San José State University.

I chose Data Science as my specialization because, I could really see the impact of data-driven products in day-to-day life. I was really curious to know how Siri works, how Amazon recommends products and how in the world an autonomous vehicle can detect objects! Here I am after my graduation, ready to contribute towards the developement of incredible products and learn along the way.
